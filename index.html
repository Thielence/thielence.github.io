Introduction

This dehumidifier can reduce the humidity of an interior space, helping to slow the growth of mold or other forms of deterioration, make a space more comfortable, and promote the health of those living and working within. It is ideal for use in bedrooms or other living areas during humid seasons, or in areas where moisture-sensitive appliances or items such as produce or paper are stored.

Purpose

This site exists to provide online documentation for this dehumidifer. Here, you can find a detailed guide on how to set up your dehumidifer, and the steps needed to safely use maintain it. Also provided are answers a list of questions often asked by customers, and measures that can be taken to handle common issues you may encounter.
